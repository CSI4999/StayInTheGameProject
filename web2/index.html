Features

Stay In The Game offers three subscription tiers to offer you flexibility in enhancing the strength of your investment portfolio. Subscribed users gain access to an AI bot that analyzes market trends and provides recommendations on when to buy, hold, or sell a particular security*. Learn more about our tiers below.

  • Tier 1
    Tier 1 subscribers gain access to the Stay In The Game platform as well as the ability to track and get recommendations for one stock.
  • Tier 2
    Tier 2 subscribers receive all Tier 1 benefits and increases the number of stocks they can track to three.     
  • Tier 3
    Tier 3 subscribers get access to all the benefits of Tier 2 and are able to track up to five stocks at once.
