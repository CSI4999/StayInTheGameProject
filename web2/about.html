Meet the creators of Stay In The Game    

Stay In The Game was created by Nikolaus Bergamin, Eric Chan, Thomas Fallon, Gabriel Gomer, Divyesh Goti, and Joshua Marinkovski in the Fall of 2021 as their Senior Capstone Project at Oakland University. This project demonstrates the skills and knowledge gained by the members through their coursework, internships, and personal projects. Read on to learn more about each member.
